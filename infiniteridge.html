INFINITE RIDGE

About the Band

Infinite Ridge is a 5-member, eclectic pop-rock Pinoy band from Bacolod City, Negros Occidental, who love classic rock, slow rock, pop-rock, hard-rock, reggae, OPM, tunog-kalye, funk, dance, and just about anything else in between.

The band formed in 2017 from a collection of music-loving romantic nerds, namely, siblings Joshua Digon (lead guitar and vocals), Justin Digon (bassist and vocals), Jamie-Ann Chloe Digon (rhythm/lead guitar and vocals), as well as Ashley (keyboard and vocals) and Jimuel (drums and vocals), who all just couldn't get enough of The Beatles, Queen, Scorpions, Led Zeppelin, Deep Purple, AC/DC, Journey, U2, Bon Jovi, Linkin Park, Maroon5, Eraserheads, Rivermaya, Kamikazee, and December Avenue.

The group's unique brand of entertainment fuses hard-hitting grooves and calming tunes and tight 5-part vocal harmonies with moves like you wouldn't expect, as well as surprising set changes that will make you want to dance all day and all night long.

Now hailed as 'young musicians with old souls' by their viewing audiences, Infinite Ridge has performed in more than 100 shows and has had the pleasure of opening for iconic bands like This Band, December Avenue, Gigi de Lana, and the Gigi Vibes, and I Belong To The Zoo.

The band has also played in notable events like the Masskara Festival, Panaad Festival, Globe Loud Music Festival, Sunset Festival, Dinagsa Festival (Cadiz City), Malihaw Festival (Victorias City), Sinigayan Festival (Sagay), Hinugyaw Festival (Hinigaran), Sinulog Festival (Kabankalan), Dinagyang Festival (Iloilo City) and Puregold Negosyo Caravan.

During the month-long MassKara Festival in 2022, Infinite Ridge was fortunate enough to play in big stages like the NGC Stage, the SMB Stage, Sunset Stage, Globe Stage, and the Ayala Capitol Central Malls Corte. The band was also among the featured artists during the Masskara Festival Tattoo Convention.

In Panaad Festival this year, Infinite Ridge was also fortunate to be featured twice on the San Miguel Beer Panaad 2023 stage and in the Bacolod booth during the festivities.

The band was also the main act in various events like the Brgy. Calumangan Annual Fiesta, Tabunan Sportsfest Culminating party, Punta Taytay Fiesta de San Juan and Patik-patik Festival, Cadiz City ABC Night, Annual Fiesta in Barangays Nabali-an and To-oy in Himamaylan City, Brgy Miranda in Pontevedra, and various Alumni Homecoming events like the CHMSU Grand Alumni Homecoming, BCNHS General Alumni Homecoming, and Pontevedra Elementary School Alumni Homecoming.

Infinite Ridge also was the featured band during the PanAsiatic Quality Control Department Christmas Party and Central Negros Electric Cooperative Christmas Party.

The band also plays regularly at Tippy’s Bistro every Saturday, and at Mr. Brew, on Sundays.

In adherence to the band’s motto of Extende Ad Astra (Reach for the stars), Infinite Ridge has come up with several original compositions now posted online and is working on several more to be released soon.

Band Members

The band consists of three siblings: Joshua Digon on lead guitar and vocals, Justin Digon on bass and vocals, and Jamie Digon on rhythm/lead guitar and vocals. They are joined by Ashley Catuiran on keyboard and vocals, and Jimuel Divinagracia on drums and vocals. Together, they create a harmonious blend of instruments and voices, showcasing their unique musical abilities.

Under Joshua's leadership, the band delivers powerful performances, combining his electrifying guitar skills with his commanding vocals. Supported by Justin's solid basslines, Jamie's soulful melodies, Ashley's atmospheric keyboard textures, and Jimuel's rhythmic prowess, Infinite Ridge creates a harmonious blend of sound that is both exhilarating and emotionally charged.
